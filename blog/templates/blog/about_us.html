WHO ARE WE?

{{ post.date_added }}

Helping Hand is a simple platform by The Good Writer in partnership with Coding With Che aimed at empowering local citizens of Zimbabwe to share some of the intricate stories of their day to day lives.

WHY SHARE?

{{ post.date_added }}

Mental health being one of the pressing issues in today's society, we want people to be able to find help on our platform mainly in the form of advice from the comments. We want people to open up and show other users that they are not alone in whatever they are fighting, and of course we want them to be better at the end of the day.

WHY US?

{{ post.date_added }}

With the increase of ghost accounts on the popular social sites, it came to my realization that at times people don't want to be known on social media, their personal details, their browsing data (which is definitely being monitored by the big social companies) . Thats why we made this service such that the users are not known (we do not allow for creation of accounts) ensuring that users will maximize their sharing since there is no fear of judgement.
